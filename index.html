Discord Community Guidelines

么 Law's Basement

To maintain a positive and welcoming environment, all members must follow these rules

Last updated: March 21, 2025

1. Discord Compliance & General Conduct

Follow Discord's Terms of Service & Community Guidelines

Our rules adhere to Discord's official policies. Violations may result in a permanent ban.
Client mods like BetterDiscord and other are allowed and may be discussed freely.

Punishment: Request
Discussing Discord features and following community guidelines
Sharing exploits or content that violates Discord's TOS
Age Requirement

Members must be 13 years or older (or meet their country's minimum age requirement).
Falsifying your age, even as a joke, will result in a ban.

Punishment: Warning
No Automation, Exploits, or Harmful Tools

The use of self-bots, nitro snipers, auto-changing statuses, API exploits, or any tools that harm Discord's servers is prohibited. Violations will lead to a ban.

Punishment: Ban
Using standard Discord features and approved bots
Using self-bots, nitro snipers, or API exploits
No Raids, Doxxing, or Swatting

Do not joke about or plan raids, doxxing, or swatting. Sharing personal or doxxing information even "for awareness" is forbidden.
Historical discussions about these topics are allowed but must not promote or encourage such behavior.

Punishment: Mute
No Scams or Malicious Content

Scams, malware, viruses, IP grabbers, or malicious links are strictly prohibited.
Discussions about malware are allowed only if they do not include sharing or promoting harmful content.

Punishment: Ban
Discussing cybersecurity topics in an educational context
Sharing links to phishing sites or malware downloads
No Leaking Private Information

Do not share personal information (e.g., IPs, addresses, phone numbers, legal names, government IDs). Even fake information posted as a joke is not allowed.

Punishment: Warning
Voice Channel Recording

Recording Voice Channels without the consent of all participants is prohibited, except for owner-hosted stage events.
By participating, you agree to comply with Discord's privacy policies and relevant laws.

Punishment: Request

2. NSFW, Gore & Explicit Content

No NSFW Content

Explicit, pornographic, or sexually suggestive content is not allowed in any channel. This includes text, images, videos, links, emotes, and profile pictures.
Discussions about sexuality and gender identity are permitted when relevant to the conversation and handled maturely.

Punishment: Ban
Discussing topics related to sexuality in a respectful, educational context
Sharing explicit images or making sexually suggestive comments
No Gore or Graphic Violence

Content depicting real or extreme fictional violence, gore, animal cruelty, or death is prohibited.
Discussions about these topics are allowed in appropriate contexts without sharing graphic content.

Punishment: Mute
No Sexualization of Minors

Zero tolerance for content sexualizing minors, including fictional characters portrayed as underage.
This includes lolicon, shotacon, and any content that sexualizes or romanticizes minors.

Punishment: Ban

3. Respectful Behavior & Communication

Respect All Members

Treat all members with respect. Harassment, bullying, threats, hate speech, or discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, nationality, disability, or other personal characteristics is not tolerated.

Punishment: Warning
Engaging in respectful debate while acknowledging different viewpoints
Using slurs or making discriminatory comments about someone's identity
No Offensive or Harmful Content

Content that promotes self-harm, suicide, eating disorders, or other harmful behaviors is prohibited.
Discussions about mental health are welcome when approached responsibly and supportively.

Punishment: Request
No Controversial Topics

Avoid discussions about controversial political, religious, or divisive topics that may lead to heated arguments.
Casual mentions are fine, but extended debates on these topics should be taken to DMs.

Punishment: Mute
Briefly mentioning a political event without starting a debate
Starting heated political arguments or religious debates in public channels

4. Spam, Nuisance & Moderator Interference

No Spam or Flooding

Refrain from flooding chat or Voice Channels with repetitive messages, excessive emojis, unsolicited links, or needless pings.
Spamming includes abusing soundboards, joining/leaving repeatedly in VC, and TTS abuse.

Punishment: Warning
Sending messages at a reasonable pace that contribute to the conversation
Sending multiple short messages in quick succession or spamming emotes
No Disruptive Voice Chat Behavior

Voice Chat disruptions (e.g., mic spam, earrape, voice changers, background noise) are prohibited.
Mute your mic when not speaking if you have background noise.

Punishment: Mute
Respect Moderator Decisions

Moderator decisions are final. Do not argue with moderators in public channels or attempt to bypass punishments.
If you believe a decision was unfair, appeal through DMs or the designated appeal process.

Punishment: Ban
Respectfully appealing a decision through proper channels
Arguing with moderators in public channels or creating alt accounts to bypass bans

5. Language & Profiles

English Only

English is the primary language for all channels. Brief phrases in other languages are acceptable, but extended conversations should be in English to maintain inclusivity.

Punishment: Request
Appropriate Profiles

Profile pictures, banners, and usernames must be appropriate and not contain NSFW content, hate symbols, or offensive material.
Moderators may ask you to change inappropriate profile elements.

Punishment: Warning
Using a profile picture that is appropriate for all ages
Using suggestive profile pictures or offensive usernames

6. Advertising & Rule Loopholes

No Advertising

Unsolicited advertising of Discord servers, social media, products, or services is prohibited.
Sharing content when relevant to a discussion is allowed, but avoid self-promotion.

Punishment: Warning
Sharing a relevant video when it adds to the conversation
Repeatedly posting links to your YouTube channel or Discord server
No Rule Loopholes

Attempting to find loopholes in the rules or exploiting technicalities will be treated as a violation.
The spirit of the rules is as important as their literal interpretation.

Punishment: Ban

These rules are subject to change at any time. It is your responsibility to stay updated with the current rules.
By participating in this server, you agree to follow these rules and accept the consequences of breaking them.
