Discord Community Guidelines

么 Law's Community

To maintain a positive and welcoming environment, all members must follow these rules

Last updated: March 20, 2025

General Conduct

Terms of Service

Our rules are based on Discord's official policies. Breaking these is grounds for a permanent ban. This includes a strict prohibition against content that violates Discord's Terms of Service, particularly regarding harassment, hate speech, and illegal activities.

Age Requirement

You must be at least 13 years old (or meet your country's minimum) to join. Falsifying your age—even as a joke—will get you banned. This age requirement is in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service.

Content Restrictions

No NSFW content meant to sexualize or arouse is allowed. Extreme, disturbing, or gore-related content is strictly forbidden. No flirting or e-dating due to the presence of minors.

Interactions & Behavior

Respectful Communication

Treat everyone courteously - no trolling, toxicity, or behavior meant to disrupt chat. No hate speech including racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia. No death threats, even if "joking".

Chat Etiquette

Do not flood chat or VC with repetitive messages, excessive emojis, or unsolicited links. Respect channel topics and keep discussions relevant to each channel's purpose.

Language & Identity

This server is strictly English only - use translation tools if needed. Your username, profile picture, bio, and pronouns must comply with server rules. No impersonating Law, staff, or any notable figure (including bots/YouTubers).

Twitch Stream Guidelines

么 Law's Stream Chat

Follow these rules to keep our stream chat fun and engaging for everyone

Last updated: March 20, 2025

Chat Behavior

Positive Atmosphere

Keep a positive atmosphere in chat. Support other viewers and the streamer. No backseat gaming unless requested by the streamer. Let Law play the game their way!

Prohibited Content

No spam, excessive caps, or emoji spam. No self-promotion unless explicitly allowed by the streamer. No discussion of controversial/divisive topics including politics, religion, or current events that may disrupt the stream.

Links & External Content

Do not post links without permission. No unsolicited advice about streaming equipment, games, or other technical aspects unless specifically asked for.

Moderation & Enforcement

Moderator Decisions

Moderator decisions are final. Do not argue with moderators in chat. If you have concerns about moderation, send a private message to Law after the stream.

Rule Violations

Rule violations will result in warnings, timeouts, or permanent bans depending on severity and frequency. Attempts to evade bans will result in immediate reporting to Twitch.

Community Protection

Help protect our community by reporting inappropriate behavior to moderators. Use channel points responsibly and as intended.
